Latest Scam Alerts

Stay informed about common scam techniques and emerging online threats.

New
Fake Banking Messages

Fraudulent text messages claiming to be from banks requesting account verification.

Alert
Investment Fraud

High-return investment opportunities promoted through social media and messaging apps.

Trending
Package Delivery Scams

Fake shipping notifications asking recipients to click malicious links.

Warning
Technical Support Scams

Unsolicited calls claiming your device is infected and requires immediate payment.

Cyber Safety Tips

Simple habits can significantly reduce your exposure to cyber threats.

Enable MFA

Protect accounts with multi-factor authentication whenever available.

Verify Emails

Carefully inspect unexpected emails before clicking links or downloading attachments.

Never Share OTPs

Legitimate organizations will never ask for one-time verification codes.

Keep Software Updated

Install security updates promptly to reduce known vulnerabilities.
